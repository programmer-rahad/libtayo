The REACH Patient Support Program

Sanofi Genzyme is committed to supporting patients who have been recently prescribed LIBTAYO for adult patients with metastatic or locally advanced CSCC
who are not candidates for curative surgery or curative radiation.

The REACH Patient Support Program is designed to provide eligible patients with services like medication administration,
reimbursement or financial assistance, navigation support, and education.

The REACH Patient Support Program
is committed to helping your patients get the most out of LIBTAYO

Medication Access Navigation

REACH acts as the single point of contact between healthcare professionals and patients, facilitating patient access to LIBTAYO treatment in clinics.

Reimbursement or Financial Assistance

REACH provides support in navigating reimbursement or financial assistance options for patients.

Education

REACH provides patients with treatment education materials before their first dose and are available for any questions related to patient care and treatment.
