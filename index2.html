LIBTAYO (cemiplimab) is indicated for the treatment of adult patients with metastatic or locally advanced cutaneous squamous cell carcinoma who are not candidates for curative surgery or curative radiation.

LIBTAYO has been issued marketing authorization with conditions, pending the results of trials to verify its clinical benefit. Patients should be advised of the nature of the authorization. For further information for LIBTAYO please refer to Health Canada’s Notice of Compliance with conditions - drug products website. Marketing authorization with conditions is based on tumour response rate and durability of response. An improvement in overall survival (OS) or progression free survival (PFS) has not yet been established in this single-arm study.

Important Safety Information

Clinical use:

The safety and efficacy of LIBTAYO in pediatric patients have not been established.

Contraindications:

  • Hypersensitivity to LIBTAYO or any ingredient in the formulation or component of the container

Relevant warnings and precautions:

  • Risks of solid organ transplant rejection
  • Immune-mediated adverse reactions including:
    • - Pneumonitis
    • - Colitis
    • - Hepatitis
    • - Endocrinopathies (hypothyroidism, hyperthyroidism, hypophysitis, adrenal insufficiency, type 1 diabetes mellitus)
    • - Skin adverse reactions
    • - Nephritis
    • - Other potential immune-mediated adverse reactions, including cases of graft-versus-host disease in association with allogeneic hematopoietic stem cell transplant
  • Infusion-related reactions
  • Fertility
  • Pregnant and nursing women
  • Elderly
  • Driving or operating machinery

For more information:
